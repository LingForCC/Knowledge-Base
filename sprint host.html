Sprint Host

Sprint Host is one of the Roles in Scrum. The responsiblity for this role is to conduct Sprint activities, including but not limited to Sprint Planning Meeting, Daily Stand Up, Requirement Refinement Meeting, Retrospective, Sprint Review and so on. The reason we need this role is mainly because we would like to setup the ownership within the team.

 

Who

Sprint Host is usually took within the team in rotation manner.

 

Responsibility

The responsiblity includes conducting following Sprint activities

1. Sprint Planning Meeting
This is a meeting the team gather to give out the commitment for a Sprint. It usually happens on the first day of a Sprint.

2. Daily Stand Up
This is a meeting the team gather to share the progress or find neccessary help. It usually happens in the morning every day.

3. Requirement Refinement Meeting
This is a meeting the team gather with product manager to discuss the things which will be implemented in next 1-2 sprints. It happens as long as there are things to discuss.

4. Retrospective
This is a meeting the team gather to see what were done well/bad for the current Sprint.

5. Sprint Review
This is a meeting the team gather with product manager and stakeholders to do the demo and collect feedbacks.

Besides of meetings, Sprint Host is also responsible for

  • Identify Risks and Obstacles
  • Account for External Dependencies
