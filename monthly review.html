Monthly Review

In order to connect with the employees and understand their latest thoughts, it is important to have a monthly review with your employee.

Schedule

Usually the manager can go through a couple of questions as mentioned below to trigger the discussions. The manager should share these questions with the employees so employees can do some preparations before the review.

Retrospective
What is the biggest accomplishment in last month? Does it comply with company/team goal? Does it comply with individual goal?

Future
Is the company/team goal clear enough for next couple of months? Does it help to improve myself and move forward in my career path?

If yes then

  • What is the biggest challenge right now?
  • What things should we do differently?
  • What resources would be helpful?

If no

  • Why is it?
  • What can we do to change it?

Open Discussion
Any relevant topic which is not mentioned before.

Notice that usually we can use the first two sections to trigger the discussion or find more things to discuss.

After Review

It is also important to take down notes during the review and do follow ups later.
