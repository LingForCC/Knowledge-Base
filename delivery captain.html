Delivery Captain

Theoretically, the team can focus on the things prioritized by PM in current or next sprint if the team is running in Scrum. But practically, we should also focus on big things which require 1 or 2 quarters to complete. That is the reason we need a dedicated person who focuses on the release, which includes several sprints.

 

Who

Delivery Captain is usually took within the team in rotation way.

 

How to Define Release

A release is one or more Epics which requires 1 or 2 quarters to complete. Usually a deadline is required for a release.

 

Responsibility

Delivery Captain needs to ensure the team meets the big things(The Epics) while the team is running in Scrum manner.

1. Estimation and Plan
Lead the team to do large scale estimation and setup the plan.

2. Track the Progress
Track the progress in real time to make sure unexpected requirements or other risks won’t fail the ultimate goal.

3. Communicatin Changes
Once there is any change in the plan, it is Delivery Captain's responsiblity to communicat this with stakeholders.
